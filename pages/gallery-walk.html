What to Display

Station Must-Haves

Station Display
  • Project title & group name (Team Warisan, etc.)
  • Cultural heritage challenge identified
  • Artisan user insight and POV statement
  • AI-driven solution and prototype
  • SDG 11 impact statement
  • SMART DT App screenshot (T13 evidence)
Presenter Script (1 min)
  1. Who is the user? Your artisan persona
  2. What is the problem? Cultural heritage challenge
  3. What is your solution? AI-driven answer
  4. Show the prototype — demo or mockup
  5. Impact — SDG 11 + community benefit
Feedback Collection

Collect peer and judge feedback at your station using:

  • Sticky notes (3 strengths, 1 suggestion)
  • Google Form QR code (if provided)
  • Verbal Q&A from visiting judges

All feedback feeds into T14 (Feedback Form).

During the Walk

Group Roles at the Station

Group Leader

Manages station flow. Welcomes visitors. Ensures all members take turns presenting.

Pitch Lead

Delivers the 1-minute station pitch. Handles judge Q&A with confidence.

Evidence Team

Photographs and videos every interaction. Captures feedback sticky notes for T14.

App Recorder

Enters gallery walk evidence into SMART DT App. Confirms T13 is complete before session starts.

Judging During Gallery Walk

Judges will visit each station during the walk. They may ask questions about your cultural insight, AI solution and impact plan. This is a formative assessment — not the final pitch. Final scoring happens on Day 4.

Tip: Know your HMW question and POV statement by heart. Judges often ask about these first.

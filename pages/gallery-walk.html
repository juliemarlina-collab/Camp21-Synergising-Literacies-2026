Heritage Exhibition & Peer Feedback

Heritage Gallery Walk

A showcase of cultural insights, AI-driven solutions, prototypes and Design Thinking evidence.

Station Setup

What Every Group Must Display

Each station should communicate the full journey from cultural insight to AI-powered impact in 1 minute.

1 · Heritage Problem

Problem + User Insight

  • Project title and group name
  • Cultural heritage challenge
  • Artisan/user quote
  • POV statement and HMW question
2 · AI Solution

Prototype + How It Works

  • AI-driven solution concept
  • Sketch/mockup/model/demo
  • Key features
  • SMART DT App screenshots
3 · Impact

SDG + Feedback

  • SDG 11 impact statement
  • Stakeholders and beneficiaries
  • Feedback QR/sticky note area
  • Improvement action
Presenter Script

1-Minute Station Explanation

20 sec: Our heritage problem and user insight.
20 sec: Our AI-powered solution and prototype.
20 sec: Expected impact, SDG 11 link and what feedback we need.

Trainer Checklist

Before Gallery Opens

  • Station title visible
  • Prototype or visual mockup ready
  • One presenter stays at station
  • Feedback collection method ready
  • SMART DT evidence captured
Rotation Plan

Suggested Movement Flow

1
Round 1
Groups 1–3 visit Groups 4–6. Groups 4–6 present.
2
Round 2
Groups 4–6 visit Groups 1–3. Groups 1–3 present.
3
Judge / Trainer Walkthrough
Judges and trainers visit all stations for observation, feedback and quality check.
Student Output

Feedback notes, photos, improvement list and pitch refinement.

Judge Output

Initial observations, possible Q&A questions and innovation highlights.

Organiser Output

Gallery photos, station evidence and reporting highlights.

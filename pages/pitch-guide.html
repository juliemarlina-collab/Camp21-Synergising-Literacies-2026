Pitch Guide

A strong slide-by-slide pitch framework for AI and cultural heritage preservation.

Visionary Pitch

7–8 Slide Structure

The pitch must convince a global panel that the AI-driven solution can preserve cultural heritage sustainably.

1. Title

Hook, team and initiative name.

2. Challenge

Define the cultural heritage issue.

3. Solution

Present the AI-driven value proposition.

4–5. How It Works

Use prototype, mockup, AI visual or 15–20s video.

6. Implementation

Stakeholders, resources and roadmap.

7. Impact

Short-term, long-term and SDG 11 impact.

8. Closing

Call to action and inspiring final statement.

Q&A

Prepare for feasibility, AI, SDG and scaling questions.

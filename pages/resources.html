What Each Template Asks

DT Template Reference T01–T16

All templates are submitted in the SMART DT App. Reference this if you are unsure what a template requires.

Phase 01–02 · Empathise & Define
T01 Interview Guide & Notes — artisan interview questions and raw answers
T02 Empathy Map — what artisan thinks, feels, says, does
T03 Persona Card — fictional profile of your user
T04 Needs Summary — top 3 unmet needs
T05 POV Statement — [user] needs [need] because [insight]
T06 HMW Question — How Might We solve this?
Phase 03 · Ideate
T07 Brainstorming — minimum 100 raw ideas
T08 SCAMPER — Substitute, Combine, Adapt, Modify, Put to other use, Eliminate, Reverse
T09 Idea Matrix — rate ideas by feasibility & impact
T10 Final Idea Selection — chosen concept with justification
Phase 04–05 · Prototype & Test
T11 Prototype Plan — what to build, materials, tools
T12 Version Log — document each prototype iteration
T13 Evidence Photos — visual proof of every stage
T14 Feedback Form — test results from peers & gallery walk
T15 Improvement Plan — changes based on feedback
T16 Reflection — group learning and what you would do differently

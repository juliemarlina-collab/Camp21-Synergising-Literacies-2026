During the Programme

Red Flag Alert Guide

🔴 Alert

Group Stuck on Empathy

Students cannot conduct interviews. Action: Trainer demonstrates one interview with an artisan. Assign T01 as example first.

⚠️ Watch

App Not Submitting

Student cannot save template. Action: Contact Tech/App Support immediately. Use photo evidence as backup.

📋 Note

Pitch Not Ready

Group behind on Day 3. Action: Escalate to Lead Trainer. Reduce slides to 5 minimum. Focus on SDG 11 alignment.

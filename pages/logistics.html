Jalan Conlay, Kuala Lumpur. Main venue for gallery tours, workshops and pitching.

The Conlay MRT Station is directly opposite Kompleks Kraf. Highly recommended.

📍 Pick-up Points

Pick-up service will be provided. Designated points:

  • Avenue K Drop-Off Bay — Jalan Ampang entrance
  • Pavilion KL Bus / Coach Bay — Jalan Bukit Bintang side, near Pavilion Kuala Lumpur

The list of pick-up points will be updated from time to time.

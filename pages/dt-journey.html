5-Phase Human-Centred Process

Design Thinking Journey

Transforming ideas into sustainable cultural heritage solutions — from empathy to pitch.

SMART DT App Journey

Use the app as the main group documentation tool. Each group submits one complete journey from Empathy to Pitch.

Required Outputs per Phase
  • T01–T04: Interview, empathy map, persona and needs summary.
  • T05–T06: POV statement and How Might We question.
  • T07–T10: Brainstorming, SCAMPER, idea matrix and final idea.
  • T11–T13: Prototype plan, version log and evidence.
  • T14–T16: Feedback, improvement plan and reflection.
Phase by Phase

What Each Phase Looks Like at Camp21

Phase 01 · Empathise

Batik Discovery

Visit the craft gallery. Interview batik artisans. Observe, listen and document real cultural insights before proposing any solution.

Phase 03 · Ideate

Wau Innovation Studio

Generate imaginative AI-powered solutions rooted in cultural symbols. Use SCAMPER, mind-mapping and concept sketching.

Phase 04 · Prototype

Craft Prototype Studio

Build low-fidelity mockups, AI-generated visuals or short video simulations that bring your heritage solution to life.

Pitch Guide
A 7–8 slide persuasive pitch for an AI-driven solution that preserves Malaysian cultural heritage.
Slide Structure
SlideContentPurpose
1Title & Team IntroductionHook and identity.
2Cultural Heritage ChallengeProblem and urgency.
3AI-Driven SolutionValue proposition.
4–5How It WorksPrototype, AI visual, video or before/after story.
6Implementation & StakeholdersRoadmap and resources.
7Expected Impact & SDG 11Sustainability and measurable impact.
8Call to ActionStrong closing.
AI must be part of the solution, not only used to decorate slides.

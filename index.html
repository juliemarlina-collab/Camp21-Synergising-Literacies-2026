About CAMP21

Modern literacy, cultural intelligence and AI-powered innovation.

CAMP21 2026 blends English communication, Malaysian cultural heritage exploration, Design Thinking, AI empowerment and sustainable development goals through guided activities and team-based challenges.

01

Culture

Explore craft, identity, symbols and local stories.

02

Innovation

Turn insights into AI-supported heritage solutions.

Participant Journey

Register, draw lot, discover your group and begin the challenge.

Participants will register, join the draw-lot process and immediately know their assigned group. The website can later display group allocation and progress automatically.

01

Register

Participants complete registration and attendance verification.

02

Draw Lot

Participants draw lots and are assigned into 10 official CAMP21 teams.

03

Find My Group

Team name, group code and members are displayed in the Participant Hub.

04

Complete DT Tasks

Groups complete Smart DT tasks, upload evidence and receive trainer feedback.

05

Pitch & Reflect

Teams prepare 7–8 slides, pitch their AI heritage solution and reflect on feedback.

Official Group Names

10 teams × 6 participants.

CAMP21 Design Thinking Journey

Five guided phases from cultural insight to sustainable impact.

01

Empathise

Interview users, observe cultural context and understand artisan/community needs.

02

Define

Frame the cultural heritage challenge into a clear problem statement.

03

Ideate

Generate creative AI-supported solutions inspired by heritage and SDGs.

04

Prototype

Build sketches, mockups, models, storyboards or digital concepts.

05

Test

Collect feedback, improve the idea and prepare for final pitching.

SDG Impact

Designing cultural heritage solutions with sustainable purpose.

CAMP21 connects AI and Design Thinking with SDG 4, SDG 11 and SDG 12 so participants learn, preserve and innovate responsibly.

SDG 4

Quality Education

Build communication, creativity, AI literacy and collaborative learning.

SDG 11

Sustainable Cities & Communities

Support cultural heritage preservation and community-centred innovation.

SDG 12

Responsible Consumption

Encourage sustainable materials, ethical design and responsible preservation.
